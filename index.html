I’m a Cybersecurity and Risk Management professional with a Master’s degree in Computer Science and over five years of experience helping organizations secure their systems, stay compliant, and reduce risk. I’ve worked extensively with standards like NIST, PCI-DSS, and ISO 8583, especially in financial and enterprise environments.
